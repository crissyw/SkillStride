Our Approach

Focus on mastery

Every student learns at her own pace so we don’t rush materials for the sake of completion.

Emphasize on what’s practical

We want what we teach to be useful, so we tailor our teaching to student goals and passions.

Learn by doing

We’ll do a bit of theory but, after that, we’ll jump straight into practice.

Make every lesson fun

Students will retain material that they enjoy so everything is designed with fun in mind.
