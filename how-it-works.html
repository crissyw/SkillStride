Our Approach

Focus on mastery

Every student learns at her own pace so we don’t rush materials for the sake of completion.

Emphasize on what’s practical

We want what we teach to be useful, so we tailor our teaching to student goals and passions.

Learn by doing

We’ll do a bit of theory but, after that, we’ll jump straight into practice.

Make every lesson fun

Students will retain material that they enjoy so everything is designed with fun in mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who are Kreasi’s teachers?

    Our teachers are native English-speaking teachers. They bring their own expertise and strengths to our program. We will match you to a teacher based on your needs and goals.

  • How much English do I need to know before joining Kreasi?

    We are open to all students of all proficiency levels (from beginners to advanced). We will customize your curriculum based on your needs and goals. All we need is a healthy dose of curiosity and hunger for learning.

  • What age groups do you accept?

    We accept students of age 6-18 (SD/Primary School to SMA/High School).

  • How much does it cost?

    Our service costs much less than what you would normally pay. Please reach out to us so our advisors can help you choose the right learning format that suits your needs.

  • How long is a lesson?

    Each lesson is an interactive 40 minute session with a native English-speaking teacher. A lesson consists of an overview of key concepts, in-class exercises and live practice with the teacher.

  • How long does it take to complete a course?

    While a course typically takes between 4-6 months, we don’t set a definite timeline to our students. We believe that each student learns at her own pace. We don’t want to rush through materials for the sake of completion.

  • In a group lesson format, how big are the classrooms?

    We keep our group size to fewer than 10 students to ensure a high level of engagement.

  • What technology or tools do I need to have?

    All you need is access to a computer, a tablet, or a smartphone, and a stable internet connection.

  • Do your coaches help with your school homework?

    When you join Kreasi, we will develop a curriculum for you. We are more than happy to devote a limited portion of class time to answer questions on school assignments. However, most class time will be spent on your own learning plan.

  • How can I find out more about Kreasi’s curriculum?

    Feel free to reach out at info@kreasi.io
    We are more than happy to provide some curriculum examples.
